How do I save my game?
Your game automatically saves when you exit or when the app goes to background! Just relaunch Iodoku and click "Resume Game" on the landing page to continue where you left off.
How do I remove ads?
Upgrade to Premium in the app! Go to Settings ‚Üí Premium to subscribe. Premium removes all ads, provides unlimited hints, and supports development.
What is the AI Tutor?
The AI Tutor explains Sudoku solving techniques in plain language. When you get a hint, tap "Ask AI Tutor" to learn WHY that move works. It's 100% local - no internet required!
What is Coach Mode?
Your personal Sudoku coach provides real-time feedback on your moves. Choose from:
  • Silent - No coaching, play independently
  • Gentle - Warnings when you make problematic moves
  • Deep Analysis - Detailed strategic guidance
How does Auto-Update Candidates work?
When enabled (Settings ‚Üí Assistance), placing a number automatically removes it from pencil marks in the same row, column, and 3√ó3 box. This helps you track possible numbers more easily!
What is Smart Learning difficulty?
Smart Learning creates puzzles tailored to YOUR skill level! The AI analyzes which techniques you've mastered and generates puzzles that help you learn new strategies.
How do I use landscape mode?
Simply rotate your phone to landscape! The puzzle and number pad will arrange side-by-side. Enable "Left-Handed Mode" in Settings ‚Üí Controls to put the number pad on the left side.
How do tooltips work?
Long press (press and hold for ~1 second) on any button to see what it does! You'll feel a gentle vibration and see a helpful tooltip explaining the feature.
Can I play offline?
Yes! All features work completely offline. The AI Tutor and Coach are 100% local - no internet connection required.
Lost my progress?
Your current game auto-saves! Click "Resume Game" on the landing page. For backup across devices, enable iCloud Sync in Settings (Premium feature).

üêõ Troubleshooting

App is frozen on "Generating Puzzle"
The generator has a 30-second timeout. If it hangs, wait for the error message and try:
  • Try again (often works on second attempt)
  • Select a slightly easier difficulty
  • Restart the app
Sound effects not playing
Check Settings ‚Üí Audio ‚Üí "Game Sounds" is turned ON. Also check your device's mute switch isn't enabled.
Resume Game button doesn't appear
This means there's no saved incomplete game. Start a new game, make some moves, then exit the app. When you relaunch, "Resume Game" will appear.
App crashes or freezes
Please email us at support@seppidesign.com with:
  • What you were doing when it crashed
  • Your device model and iOS version
  • Any error messages you saw
